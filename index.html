Using your rug

We recommend to placing the rug on top of your regular yoga mat. These rugs are designed to give you a more challenging practice during active postures and a more relaxing experience in resting postures. 

Try using the striped pattern as a guide to improve alignment. 

If you would like to increase the grip, spray lightly with water. 

Care Instructions

Machine wash in cold water with mild detergent. Air dry only to prevent shrinkage. Iron at any temperature with steam if desired.
